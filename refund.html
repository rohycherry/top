Refund Policy

We strive for customer satisfaction with fair and transparent refund policies

Our Refund Policy

At Gamelyra, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Please read this policy carefully to understand our refund procedures.

Due to the digital nature of our products, we have specific refund conditions designed to be fair to both our customers and our business.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be considered under the following circumstances:

  • The account was not delivered due to technical issues on our end
  • The account details provided do not match the description
  • The account is inaccessible within 24 hours of purchase
  • Duplicate payment for the same order

Note: All refund requests must be submitted within 24 hours of purchase and before any account details have been used or modified.

Non-Refundable Situations

Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds in these cases:

  • If you change your mind after receiving the account details
  • If the game developer bans the account (unless it was already banned at time of purchase)
  • If you provided incorrect information during purchase
  • If you've already used or modified the account credentials
  • If the request is made more than 24 hours after purchase

Chargeback Policy

Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us will result in immediate account suspension and may lead to permanent banning from our services.

Important: Please contact us first if you have any issues with your purchase. We're here to help and will work with you to resolve any problems.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact us within 24 hours of your purchase
  2. Provide your order number and reason for the refund request
  3. Do not use or modify the account credentials while your request is being reviewed
  4. Our support team will review your request and respond within 48 hours

If your refund is approved, it will be processed to your original payment method within 5-10 business days.

Account Replacement

In some cases, instead of a refund, we may offer to replace the account with one of equal or greater value if:

  • The account has minor issues that don't match the description exactly
  • You're unsatisfied but still want a similar product
  • The issue is something we can rectify with a different account

Questions About Refunds

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us before making a purchase. We're happy to clarify any concerns you might have.
