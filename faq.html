Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our game account marketplace

Purchasing & Payments

How do I purchase a game account?

To purchase a game account, simply browse our selection, choose the account you want, click "Buy Now," and complete the checkout process. You'll receive the account details instantly after payment confirmation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, cryptocurrency, and other popular payment options depending on your region.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and secure payment processors to protect your payment information. We never store your full payment details on our servers.

Account Delivery & Access

How quickly will I receive my account?

All accounts are delivered instantly after payment confirmation. You'll receive the account details via email and they'll be available in your account dashboard immediately.

What should I do after receiving the account?

After receiving the account, we strongly recommend that you immediately change the password and enable two-factor authentication if available. This helps secure the account and prevent unauthorized access.

What if the account doesn't work?

If you encounter any issues with your account, please contact our support team immediately. We provide 24/7 support and will help resolve any problems you might have.

Security & Safety

Are these accounts legitimate?

Yes, all accounts we sell are legitimate. We verify each account before listing it for sale to ensure it meets our quality standards and matches the description provided.

Is buying game accounts against the game's Terms of Service?

Many games have terms that prohibit account trading. While we ensure our accounts are legitimate, we cannot guarantee that game developers won't take action against accounts. Use at your own risk.

What happens if the account gets banned after purchase?

We're not responsible for bans that occur after the account has been delivered, especially if the ban results from actions taken after purchase. However, if an account was already banned at the time of purchase, we'll provide a replacement or refund.

Refunds & Returns

Do you offer refunds?

Due to the digital nature of our products, we generally don't offer refunds once account details have been delivered. However, we may make exceptions in cases where the account doesn't match its description or has major issues. Please see our Refund Policy for details.

What if I received the wrong account?

If you received an account that doesn't match the description, contact us immediately. We'll verify the issue and provide a replacement account or refund depending on the circumstances.

Can I exchange my account for a different one?

Exchanges are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you haven't used or modified the account, we may be able to exchange it for another account of equal value. Contact our support team for assistance.
